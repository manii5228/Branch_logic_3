Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BranchLogic

How do I apply for a job?

Simply click on any job listing, review the details, and click the "Apply Now" button. You'll need to create an account and upload your resume.

Is it free to use BranchLogic?

Yes! Job seekers can browse and apply to jobs completely free. Employers pay a small fee to post job listings.

How do I post a job as an employer?

Sign up as an employer, complete your company profile, and use the "Post Job" feature to create detailed job listings.

Can I search for remote jobs only?

Absolutely! Use the remote filter in the search bar to find only remote or work-from-home opportunities.
